Team 8838D: Amirite

We are team 8838D, Amirite, located in Irvine, California. We consist of 6 members and compete in the VEX V5 Robotics Competition. Our team has a wide array of different levels and experiences, from no robotics experience at all to our second year of VRC. We aim to utilize all the strengths of each member to create one cohesive team. Our overall mission is to get to the World Championship, and to perform well at every single competition we go to. We all strive to do well this season and to expand our knowledge in robotics. Using our collaboration and innovation skills, we aim to do our best in the future competitions to come. We will persevere and overcome any challenge as a team.
